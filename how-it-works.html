The Process

Simple steps.
Powerful results.

Whether you're starting from scratch or already have a website, our process is designed to be clear, transparent, and fast. Here's exactly what working with us looks like.

Phase 01
Discovery
& Planning

Before we write a single line of code, we understand your business inside out — your goals, your tools, and your biggest pain points.

Always free
💬
Step 1.1
Free Discovery Call

We start with a relaxed conversation — no pitch, no pressure. We ask about your business, your current processes, and where you feel you're losing the most time or missing opportunities.

✓ No commitment required
🗺️
Step 1.2
Process Mapping

We map out your current workflows end-to-end — identifying bottlenecks, manual handoffs, and the highest-impact automation opportunities specific to your business.

✓ Included in discovery
🎯
Step 1.3
Scope & Recommendation

Based on what we learn, we recommend the right combination of services — website build, automations, AI chatbot, or integrations — and explain clearly why each one will help.

✓ Tailored to your situation
Phase 02
Proposal
& Agreement

You'll know exactly what we're building, what it costs, and when it'll be done — before we start anything.

Fully transparent
📋
Step 2.1
Clear Scope of Work

We produce a detailed proposal covering exactly what we'll build, which systems we'll connect, and the full workflow architecture. No vague promises — just a clear plan.

✓ Written confirmation of everything
💷
Step 2.2
Transparent Pricing

All implementation costs and any ongoing platform licensing fees are confirmed in writing before we begin. No hidden extras, no surprises when the invoice arrives.

✓ No hidden charges — ever
⏱️
Step 2.3
Realistic Timeline

We give you a realistic delivery timeline based on the project scope — not an optimistic guess. Simple automations can be live within 48 hours. Larger builds are planned properly.

✓ Avg. first prototype in 48h
Phase 03
Build
& Test

We build everything to your agreed spec, test it thoroughly across real data, and keep you in the loop throughout.

Where the magic happens
🎨
Step 3.1
Website Build or Existing Site Connect

If you need a new site, we design and build it first — your branded foundation. If you already have a site, we connect straight to it. Either way, this is where your digital presence takes shape.

✓ Works with any existing website
⚡
Step 3.2
Automation & AI Layer

We build your workflow automations, AI chatbot, and system integrations — connecting your forms, website, CRM, finance tools, and communication platforms into one seamless pipeline.

✓ Jotform, Google Forms, webhooks & more
🧪
Step 3.3
Thorough Testing

Every workflow is tested across real data scenarios including edge cases and error conditions. We don't hand you something that only works when everything goes right.

✓ Error handling built into every flow
Phase 04
Deploy
& Handover

We go live, walk you through everything, and hand over full documentation so you're never left in the dark.

You're in control
🚀
Step 4.1
Go Live

We deploy everything to production and confirm all systems are running correctly. For websites, this includes DNS setup and performance checks. For automations, we run live trigger tests.

✓ Supervised launch every time
📖
Step 4.2
Full Documentation

You receive complete documentation of everything we've built — how it works, how to update it, and what to do if something needs changing. You'll never be dependent on us to understand your own systems.

✓ Plain English, not technical jargon
🛡️
Step 4.3
Ongoing Monitoring

We set up proactive monitoring across your website and automation endpoints. If anything fails, we're alerted immediately and get to work before you even notice. 99.9% uptime SLA.

✓ Average alert response under 2 minutes

Common Questions

Things people
ask us.

How long does a typical project take?
Simple automations can be live within 48 hours. A full website build plus automation layer typically takes 1–3 weeks depending on complexity. We give you a precise timeline during scoping.
Do I need to be technical to work with you?
Not at all. We handle everything technical. You describe what you want your business to do — we figure out how to build it. Most clients have no technical background whatsoever.
What if I only need one thing — like just a chatbot?
Absolutely fine. Every service can be taken as a standalone. You don't need a full build to work with us — we scope exactly what you need and nothing more.
What happens if something breaks after you've built it?
We monitor everything proactively. If a workflow fails, we're alerted instantly and fix it fast — often before you've even noticed. That's what the 99.9% uptime SLA is for.
Can I start small and add more later?
Yes — and many clients do. Start with one automation or a chatbot, see the impact, and expand from there. Everything we build is designed to grow with you.
Will I be locked in to using you for updates?
No. We hand over full documentation so you understand everything we've built. You're never dependent on us — though many clients choose to stay with us for ongoing maintenance because it's easier.
