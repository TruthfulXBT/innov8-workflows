Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

MarketBubbles ("we", "our", "the app") is a multi-asset portfolio visualizer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

Account Data

If you sign in with Google or email, we store your email address and display name via Firebase Authentication to enable cloud sync of your portfolios and custom assets.

Portfolio & Asset Data

Your portfolios, holdings, custom assets (property, pensions, savings, etc.) are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to Google Cloud Firestore if you are signed in. This data is only accessible to you.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage analytics (page views, feature usage) to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect financial account numbers, passwords, or banking details
  • We do not sell, share, or trade your personal data with third parties
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or microphone

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication and data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • CoinGecko API — Cryptocurrency market data (no personal data sent)
  • Financial Modeling Prep API — Stock/index data (API key stored locally on your device)
  • Stripe — Payment processing for sponsored placements. Stripe Privacy Policy

Data Storage & Security

Local data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Cloud data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore with security rules that restrict access to authenticated users only. All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Export your portfolio data from the app
  • Use the app without signing in (data stays local only)
  • Clear all local data by clearing your browser/app storage

Children's Privacy

MarketBubbles is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
