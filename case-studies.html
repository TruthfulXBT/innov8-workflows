Professional Services
Lead capture to CRM to invoice — fully automated for a consultancy firm

A UK-based management consultancy was manually processing every new enquiry — copying contact details into their CRM, creating invoices by hand in Xero, and sending follow-up emails one at a time. Each new lead took 25–30 minutes of admin.

92%
Time saved per lead
2min
Response time
The problem

Every enquiry through their Jotform contact form required someone to manually: check the form submission, copy details into HubSpot, create a contact record, draft and send a confirmation email, and create a draft invoice in Xero. With 15–20 new enquiries per week, this was consuming nearly a full day of admin time. Leads were also slipping through — enquiries received on Friday afternoons weren't followed up until Monday.
